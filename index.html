Crystals Collector

At the start of the game, a number will be given, which you will need to match.

Four magic crystals are brought forth to you each game, each is able to give a different, but unknown amount to your number.

Matching the presented number gives you the victory to keep those jewels. But, if your number exceeds the presented number, you lose ten of your jewels.

Each game, the value of the crystals changes, you must select one to find its power.
